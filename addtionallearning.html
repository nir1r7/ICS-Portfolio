Spodissey

Spodissey is one of my most recent projects outside of school. This project was a collaboration with Joshua, Aidan and Patrick, during the LyonHacks-III contest. Spodissey is a website that gets the user to log into their spotify account. From there, the user can click on any country for song recommendations that are germane to their music taste but also popular in the country. As a result of our hard word and determination, we got 2nd in the contest.
